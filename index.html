Potential Pathways

Three core tracks at Svyasa Blockchain Club — from building smart contracts to researching zero-knowledge cryptography.

Development
⌬
Development
Smart Contract Engineering
Write, audit, and deploy Solidity contracts. Master Hardhat, Foundry, and gas optimisation for production-grade on-chain code.
Exchange
⟡
Exchange
DeFi Protocols & AMMs
Study Aave money markets, and learn to build and interact with DEX liquidity pools on-chain.
Research
⊕
Research
Zero-Knowledge & Cryptography
Explore ZK-SNARKs, STARKs, Merkle proofs, and the mathematics behind private, verifiable blockchain computation at scale.
Security
⚠
Security
Smart Contract Auditing
Identify reentrancy, overflow, and access-control vulnerabilities. Learn to use Slither, Mythril, and manual review techniques to harden production contracts.
Growth
◈
Growth
Web3 Marketing & Growth
Build community, drive adoption, and grow protocols. Master tokenomics storytelling, on-chain analytics, and conversion funnels for crypto-native audiences.
Finance
⟲
Finance
Crypto Trading & Strategy
Read candlestick charts, apply technical indicators, and build algorithmic trading bots. Understand market microstructure, risk management, and on-chain signals.
